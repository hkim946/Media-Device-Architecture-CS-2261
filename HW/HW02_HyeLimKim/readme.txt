PONG GAME

PRESS BUTTONS A AND B TO GO UP AND DOWN FOR THE RIGHT RECTANGLE/PADDLE,
AND PRESS BUTTONS UP AND DOWN TO GO UP AND DOWN FOR THE LEFT RECTANGLE/PADDLE.
** On a Mac, for button A, press L, and for button B, press K. 
    For button Up, press W, and for button Down, press S.

A POINT WILL BE ADDED TO THE OPPONENT IF A PLAYER FAILS TO BOUNCE THE BALL OFF THEIR PADDLE.

THE PADDLE WILL CHANGE COLORS EVERY TIME A PLAYER SUCCESSFULLY BOUNCES OFF THE BALL.

WHOEVER REACHES 3 POINTS FIRST WINS THE GAME.

WHEN THE BALL STOPS AND PLAYERS CAN NO LONGER MOVE THE PADDLES, THE GAME IS OVER.
